Tom swept his brush daintily back and forth--stepped back to note
the effect--added a touch here and there--criticised the effect again
--Ben watching every move and getting more and more interested, more and more absorbed.
Presently he said:
"Say, Tom, let ME whitewash a little."
Tom considered, was about to consent; but he altered his mind:
"No--no--I reckon it wouldn't hardly do, Ben.
You see, Aunt Polly's awful particular about this fence--right here on the street,
you know --but if it was the back fence I wouldn't mind and SHE wouldn't."
